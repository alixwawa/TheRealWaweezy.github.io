Biography

Alix Israel Wawa aka Waweezy is an American DJ/Producer with deep routes in experimental bass music and hip-hop. Alix grew up in a small town called Bellevue in Nashville, TN where he spent most of his adult life. Mainly a hip hop fan he first experienced the electronic music vibe from Kid Cudi's first album "Man on the Moon". Shorly after his high school graudation he quickly became attracted to the undeground edm scene attending festivals like imagine music festival and electric forest. Influenced strongly by Bassnectar, Zeds Deads, and Nero.
